# Multi-Turn Conversation Prompt Examples

Below are example multi-turn prompts for LLM evaluation. These can be used to test context retention, instruction following, and conversational flow.

---

## Example 1: Booking a Flight

**User:** Hi, I want to book a flight from New York to London next Friday.
**Assistant:** Sure! What time would you prefer to depart?
**User:** I’d like to leave in the evening.
**Assistant:** Great. Do you have a preferred airline?
**User:** No preference, just the cheapest option.
**Assistant:** Understood. I’ll look for the most affordable evening flights from New York to London next Friday. Would you like a direct flight?
**User:** Yes, direct is best.
**Assistant:** Got it. Here are some direct evening flights...

---

## Example 2: Tech Support

**User:** My laptop won’t connect to Wi-Fi.
**Assistant:** I’m sorry to hear that. Is this a new issue or has it happened before?
**User:** It’s new. Started today.
**Assistant:** Have you tried restarting your laptop?
**User:** Yes, but it didn’t help.
**Assistant:** Are other devices able to connect to the same Wi-Fi network?
**User:** Yes, my phone works fine.
**Assistant:** Let’s try forgetting the network and reconnecting. Would you like instructions?
**User:** Yes, please.
**Assistant:** Here’s how to forget and reconnect to a Wi-Fi network...

---

## Example 3: Restaurant Recommendation

**User:** Can you recommend a good Italian restaurant nearby?
**Assistant:** Absolutely! May I know your location?
**User:** I’m in downtown Chicago.
**Assistant:** Great. Do you prefer casual or fine dining?
**User:** Casual is fine.
**Assistant:** Here are some casual Italian restaurants in downtown Chicago...

---

## Example 4: Medical Appointment Scheduling

**User:** I need to schedule a doctor’s appointment for next week.
**Assistant:** Of course. What day works best for you?
**User:** Wednesday or Thursday.
**Assistant:** Morning or afternoon?
**User:** Afternoon.
**Assistant:** What type of doctor do you need to see?
**User:** A dermatologist.
**Assistant:** I’ll check available afternoon slots for a dermatologist next Wednesday and Thursday...

---

These examples can be expanded or customized for specific evaluation scenarios.
